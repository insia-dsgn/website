about me!


insia: /ɪnsiʌ/ human being, humanity.


I'm an undergraduate student from Harlem, New York majoring in Cognitive Science with a concentration in language and mind, and Design with a minor in Urban Education.


Derived from the Bengali and Arabic words ইনসান and إِنْسَان, my name is perhaps the best explanation of who I am and what I stand for. I'm seeking opportunities to use illustration and human-centered design to benefit humanity.


In my free time, I love improvising crochet pieces and painting.
